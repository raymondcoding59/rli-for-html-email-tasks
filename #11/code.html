Celebrating Michelin Guide Restaurants Using Marsh Hen Mill

We’re thrilled to share some exciting news from the culinary world. Recently, the Michelin Guide released its first-ever Guide to the American South. Naturally, we cannot help but celebrate a few of the restaurants mentioned in the guide that use our grains in their kitchens.

These chefs inspire us daily, taking the grains we grow here on Edisto Island and transforming them into dishes that earn global acclaim. It’s an honor to play even a small part in their craft.
